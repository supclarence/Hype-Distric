Our Objectives:

Hype Distric is a Filipino-made brand focused on creating stylish, comfortable, and high-quality streetwear. We want people to feel confident and express themselves through our designs while staying on trend. Our goal is to build a strong brand that stands out in the fashion industry, offering unique and well-crafted pieces. We aim to compete with international streetwear brands, proving that Filipino-made fashion can be just as bold and stylish. Through innovation and creativity, we strive to grow and make Malvar Street Wear a well-known name in the streetwear scene.
